Why we need to protect the REEF?

The Great Barrier Reef is one of Australia's most remarkable natural gifts. There are thousands of species of marine life and 3000 individual reefs. The Great Barrier Reef is one of the world's most famous tourist destination because of its natural beauty. It has colossal economic, social and heritage value. However, in these years global warming, overfishing, marine pollution and many other threats are affecting the biological and environment of the Great Barrier Reef. It has lost more than half of its corals since 1995 due to the climate change. Now it is our time, try our best to protect the reef!
